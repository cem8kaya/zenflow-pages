Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with ZenFlow?

Simply download the app and start your first breathing exercise or meditation session. No account or setup required! The app will guide you through each exercise with visual and haptic feedback.

Why isn't my data syncing across devices?

ZenFlow is designed to work completely offline and stores all data locally on your device for maximum privacy. This means your data stays on the device where you use the app. We don't sync data across devices because we don't use cloud servers.

How do I enable HealthKit integration?

Go to Settings â†’ HealthKit Integration and enable "Track Mindful Minutes." You'll be asked to grant permission through iOS. Your meditation sessions will then be saved to Apple Health.

Can I customize breathing exercises?

Yes! ZenFlow offers multiple breathing patterns including Box Breathing, 4-7-8, and more. You can select your preferred pattern and the app will guide you with visual animations and haptic feedback.

How do I use the Zen Garden?

Complete meditation sessions and breathing exercises to earn Zen Points. Use these points to unlock new plants, decorations, and special elements for your virtual garden. Your garden grows as your practice deepens!

Why don't I receive notifications?

Make sure you've granted notification permissions to ZenFlow in iOS Settings. Also check that you've enabled reminders in the ZenFlow app settings.

How do I reset my progress?

Go to Settings â†’ Reset Data. Note that this action cannot be undone and will delete all your local session history and progress.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! ZenFlow doesn't collect any data. Everything stays on your device. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
