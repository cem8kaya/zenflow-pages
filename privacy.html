Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 29, 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

ZenFlow is designed with your privacy as our top priority. We believe that your meditation and wellness journey is deeply personal, and we've built our app to ensure your data stays completely private and under your control.

What Makes ZenFlow Different

100% Offline Operation: ZenFlow works entirely on your device. We don't have servers, we don't collect your data, and we don't track your activity.

No Account Required: You can use all features without creating an account or providing any personal information.

Your Data Stays on Your Device: All your meditation sessions, breathing exercises, and progress are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad.

Data We Don't Collect

  • We don't collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We don't track your location
  • We don't collect usage analytics or statistics
  • We don't use third-party analytics services
  • We don't serve advertisements
  • We don't share data with anyone because we don't collect it

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and never leaves it:

  • Session History: Your meditation and breathing exercise sessions
  • Progress Tracking: Streaks, achievements, and statistics
  • Preferences: Your selected breathing patterns, ambient sounds, and app settings
  • Zen Garden: Your virtual garden progress and unlocked items

HealthKit Integration

ZenFlow can integrate with Apple HealthKit to save your mindful minutes. This integration:

  • Is completely optional - you can disable it at any time
  • Only writes data to HealthKit (mindful minutes)
  • Requires your explicit permission through iOS
  • Data is stored in Apple's Health app, not sent to ZenFlow servers (we don't have any)

Notifications

ZenFlow can send you local notifications for:

  • Breathing exercise reminders
  • Meditation session reminders
  • Daily practice encouragement

These notifications are generated locally on your device and don't involve any external servers. You can disable them at any time in Settings.

Children's Privacy

ZenFlow does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect data, no data about children is stored or transmitted.

Data Backup

Your ZenFlow data is included in your iPhone/iPad backup through iCloud or iTunes. This is handled entirely by iOS, and ZenFlow has no access to this backed-up data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally to reflect changes in the app. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
