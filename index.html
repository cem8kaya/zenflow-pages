1. Data Collection

MyZenFlow does not collect any personal data. Our app works completely offline and does not send any data to servers.

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Breathing exercise preferences
  • Zen Garden progress
  • Earned badges and streaks
  • App settings

3. HealthKit Integration

MyZenFlow can optionally integrate with Apple HealthKit:

  • Meditation durations can be saved as "Mindfulness Minutes"
  • HealthKit usage is completely optional
  • Data is only stored in Apple's Health app
  • MyZenFlow cannot access HealthKit data through servers

4. Notifications

The app may request permission for reminder notifications:

  • Notifications are completely optional
  • They are local notifications (not push notifications)
  • No data is sent to any server

5. Third-Party Services

MyZenFlow does not use any third-party analytics services:

  • No Google Analytics
  • No Facebook SDK
  • No ad networks
  • No crash reporting services

6. Data Security

Your data is secure because:

  • No data is sent to servers
  • All data is encrypted on your device (iOS encryption)
  • No internet connection required

7. Children's Privacy

MyZenFlow is rated 4+. The app does not collect data from any age group.

â“ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is ZenFlow free?

Yes, ZenFlow is completely free to use. Basic features are available to everyone. We offer a one-time lifetime payment option for premium features.

Do I need an internet connection?

No! MyZenFlow works completely offline. You can even use it in airplane mode.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. No data is sent to servers. All your data is stored locally on your device.

How does HealthKit integration work?

You can enable it from Settings > HealthKit. Your meditation sessions are automatically saved to Apple Health as "Mindfulness Minutes".

Can I customize breathing exercises?

Yes! You can adjust duration, tempo, and repetition count for each breathing exercise.

How does the Zen Garden work?

After each meditation session, your garden tree grows. There are 7 growth stages that develop based on your total meditation time.

How do notifications work?

You can set daily reminders from Settings > Notifications. Notifications are completely optional and fully local (not push notifications).

How can I purchase premium features?

You can make a one-time lifetime purchase from Settings > Premium. You get instant access to premium features.

Can I backup my data?

Yes, your data is included in iOS's automatic iCloud backup. When you switch to a new device, your data is restored.

Which iOS versions do you support?

MyZenFlow supports iOS 16.0 and later versions.
